Lydia Best

About

Lydia Best is a writer, poet and photographer based on rural Wurundjeri bushland. Her poetry has been published in ‘Prism: An Anthology’ by Clover Press (2022), her prose in 'Impressions' an online collabortive art-writing anthology published by Bowen Street Press (2024), and 'Angst' an anthology by Bowen Street Press (2024).
